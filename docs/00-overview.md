# IP12 Estate Portal — Overview

## What the platform is

IP12 Estate Portal is a unified estate agency platform that connects property listings, tenants, landlords, and agency staff through dedicated portals. It centralises listings, tenancy management, and operations in one place.

## The four portals

- **Public Listings** — Browse and search available properties; contact the agency; no login required for basic use.
- **Tenant Portal** — Tenants log in to report maintenance, message the agency, and access documents (e.g. tenancy agreements).
- **Landlord Portal** — Landlords manage their properties, view tenancy status, and communicate with the agency.
- **Admin CRM** — Agency staff manage listings, tenants, landlords, maintenance, and internal workflows.

## Long-term goal

The platform is designed to grow. Later phases may include:

- Contractor management and job dispatch
- Automation (reminders, workflows, notifications)
- Payments (rent, fees, deposits)
- Deeper analytics and reporting

Current scope focuses on MVP: listings, tenant/landlord portals, and admin CRM.
